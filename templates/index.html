Crop prediction:

A software that predicts crops in very few steps.

It is crucial that crops be planted on land with the right sort of soil for those crops. It ensures that healthy crops are produced with maximum yield. Additionally, it prevents them from being ruined by the soil's deficiency in essential nutrients. Our websites offer a function that will assist farmers in choosing the optimum location for growing particular crops.



Fertilizer prediction:

A software that predicts crops in very few steps.

When crops are harvested, important nutrients are removed from the soil, because they follow the crop and end up at the dinner table. If the soil is not replenished with nutrients through fertilizing, crop yields will deteriorate over time. Without fertilizers, nature struggles to replenish the nutrients in the soil. Therefore, we added a clever function to our system that would predict fertilizer according to the data like nitrogen level, potassium level, phosphorus level, temperature of area, type of the soil, humidity, crops of your field. .



Weather prediction:

A software that also forecasts weather.

Weather forecasting is essential for agriculture because it enables farmers to determine when it is ideal to grow or protect their crops. And in the upcoming years, its importance will only increase. The best and most complete meteorological data is gathered by our website, "Krishak Vridhhi," to produce forecasts that are incredibly accurate.
